Welcome to the Dice game!

ONE PLAYER INSTRUCTION

Roll the dice in order to get to accumulate your score. If you reach a score of 20 or higher, you win! Be careful though, a roll of 1 means you lose. Good Luck!

TWO PLAYER INSTRUCTIONS

In the two player game, Player 1 will start and roll the dice. the number on the dice will add to their score. Plaayer 1 can then choose to roll again or hold and pass the turn to player 2. Either player wins once they reach a score of 20 or higher. However a roll of 1 will reset their score and pass the turn onto the next player
